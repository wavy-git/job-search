**Score** (1-10): single composite per Rubric 1. Honestly reflects all factors including knock-outs and friction. Top of list = next to apply; bottom = consider archiving. Score does NOT include warm-path (apply-phase factor, separate).

**Statuses**:
- `on-deck` — actively pursuing (resume tailoring or outreach in progress). Floats to top of list.
- `screened` — intake screen complete, in the active pool. Default state.
- `archive-review` — flagged by screener for archive; awaiting user confirmation. Sinks to bottom.
- `applied` — submitted (row moves to applications.md).

Transitions:
- Decide to pursue → Status flips to `on-deck`.
- Submit → row moves out to applications.md as `submitted`.
- User confirms archive on an `archive-review` entry → row moves out to archive.md.
- Hard-gate failures at intake go directly to archive.md (never enter candidates.md).

**Sort order**: Status (`on-deck` → `screened` → `archive-review`), then Score descending within each Status group.
