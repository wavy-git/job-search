# VP, AI Product Management & Delivery — Lincoln Financial

Reports to SVP of AI Product & Delivery. Translates AI tech into business outcomes across insurance, annuities, retirement.

- Own end-to-end AI product portfolio/roadmaps
- Identify high-value AI use cases
- Lead and develop AI PM teams
- Lead agile development
- Coordinate across business units (Annuities, Life, Retirement Plan, Group Protection)
- Embed responsible AI principles

- 10+ years PM, including 3-5 years senior PM leadership with AI/ML or data-driven products
- Proven success delivering AI-powered products at scale
- Launching AI in regulated environments
- ML, generative AI, NLP knowledge
- Background in financial services, insurance, fintech, or similarly regulated
- Bachelor's

## Preferred
- Hands-on with LLMs, agentic AI, enterprise AI platforms
- MLOps, model monitoring
- Life insurance/annuities/retirement product experience
