## Score bands (1-5)

After applying all knock-outs and matching against pillars, score the JD on the 1-5 band:

- **5 — Top fit.** Strong on most high-impact items, no knock-outs, ≥3 pillars activated directly. Pursue immediately.
- **4 — Good fit.** Strong on most high-impact items, minor friction, ≥2 pillars activated. Pursue.
- **3 — Moderate fit.** Mixed; real friction; ≥2 pillars activated. Hold or pursue with warm path.
- **2 — Weak fit.** Multiple high-impact items missing OR stacked friction. Archive-review.
- **1 — Poor fit.** Hard knock-out OR multiple disqualifiers. Drop.

**Cap rule:** a single hard knock-out caps the score at 1 regardless of other strengths.
