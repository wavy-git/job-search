# Principal Product Manager — Circle

## Responsibilities
- Define product strategy/roadmap for critical platform components (developer efficiency, scalability)
- Lead evolution of onboarding and compliance capabilities
- Partner across compliance, legal, risk, engineering, and product teams to design systems balancing regulatory rigor with customer experience

- Hands-on experience using AI tools for prototyping and product mock generation
- Track record implementing KYB, KYC, AML technologies for global regulatory compliance
- Expertise designing compliance workflows / scalable onboarding systems

## Preferred
- RegTech, banking, scaled fintech background
