# Vice President of Product Management — Amagi

Lead product strategy for Agentic Media Operations and FAST (Free Ad-Supported Streaming Television). Portfolio-level position directing product vision across multiple products in a Business Unit.

## Responsibilities
- Develop deep expertise in media operations, FAST, streaming, content workflows
- Define product vision for agentic media operations and FAST streaming
- Drive product strategy across media operations and FAST
- Portfolio governance: prioritize, scale/sustain/sunset
- Own structured intake of customer feedback, sales, success insights
- Partner with Product Marketing on positioning, messaging, pricing
- Track product adoption, usage, customer outcomes

## Required
- 15+ years PM with streaming, FAST, or media platform experience
- Strong understanding of content operations, FAST/OTT ecosystems, ad-supported video monetization
- Proven ability to define and scale B2B SaaS product strategies

## Preferred
- Background in studio/TV network media operations

## Salary
$275,000–$315,000 USD + incentive + equity
