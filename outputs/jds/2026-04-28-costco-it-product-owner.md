# IT Product Owner — Costco GBAPS

Own and manage product strategy and backlog for Costco's new ERP/MRP system, replatforming current IBM iSeries-based core systems.

## Responsibilities
- Form backlogs of initiatives, features, user stories
- Collaborate with PMs and tech stakeholders on requirements
- Prioritize Epic backlog
- PI planning, backlog refinement, Epic planning
- Mentor junior POs
- Author user stories with acceptance criteria
- Drive UAT

## Required
- 7+ years as Product Owner
- 5+ years on Scrum/Agile teams
- 5+ years complex software development environments
- Requirements gathering, business modeling, project scoping

## Salary
Level 1: $90K-$115K; Level 2: $105K-$135K; Level 3: $130K-$160K; Level 4: $140K-$200K
