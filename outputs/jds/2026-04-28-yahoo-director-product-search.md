# Director of Product — Yahoo Search (Sports & Entertainment Verticals)

Senior individual contributor. Reports to Senior Director of Product for Search Verticals & Growth.

## Responsibilities
- Define product vision/strategy/roadmap for Sports & Entertainment search experiences
- Build engaging real-time content experiences (scores, stats, fantasy, athlete/celebrity profiles)
- Collaborate with eng, design, ML, data science, editorial, monetization
- Capitalize on major events (Super Bowl, March Madness, World Cup, Olympics, Oscars, Grammies)
- Define/track KPIs (engagement, task completion, query success, traffic, monetization)

## Required
- 10+ years PM; 4+ years in search, content platforms, or high-scale consumer products
- Shipping front-end consumer experiences (web/mobile)
- Direct search product or query-driven systems experience
- Deep passion/knowledge of sports/entertainment

## Preferred
- ML/AI familiarity (ranking, entity extraction, personalization, embeddings, LLM)
- Structured data (knowledge graphs)
- Live events / real-time content cycles

## Salary
$150,380 - $327,025
