## Company
"Leap is on a mission to decarbonize the world's electric grids" — virtual power plants (VPPs) connecting energy technologies (battery storage, EV chargers, smart thermostats) to grid flexibility programs. Privately-held, VC-backed. Remote, distributed workplace with teammates in Europe and North America.

## Responsibilities (verbatim)
- Investigate product opportunities using customer research, prototyping and go-to-market plans
- Work closely with a team of engineers, data scientists and designers to bring features to market
- Develop metrics to define expectations and measure outcomes such as operational efficiency, revenue, platform utilization and customer satisfaction
- Engage executive and functional teams in release, operational and customer support plans
- Own the roadmap for your product area, lead regular prioritization cycles and ensure the highest impact work is scoped and requirements are clearly defined for business stakeholders and engineering teams

## Required (verbatim)
- At least 5 years of product management experience delivering software products
- At least 2 years experience working in an early stage B2B startup environment
- Aptitude for building relationships with a multinational team across several time zones
- Experience analyzing data, building workflows and prototypes with AI to support product development
- Skilled at deeply understanding customer needs and supporting prioritization decisions with qualitative and quantitative insights
- Experience working in remote or hybrid environments across time zones

## Preferred
- Experience in US wholesale electricity markets, distributed energy resources, commercial energy developers, utilities, or SaaS API product development
- Background in engineering, computer science, data science, or technical discipline
