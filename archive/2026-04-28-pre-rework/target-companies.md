## Tier priority for resume tailoring effort

- **Tier A**: highest conversion, your exact background pattern
- **Tier C**: vertical SaaS with payments = SQUIRE-like, strong second priority
- **Tier G**: direct match to current NG AMI work
- **Tier B, F, H, I, J, L**: solid matches, apply when roles posted
- **Tier D, E, K**: volume plays, lower per-application conversion
- **Tier M, N, O**: founder-operator plays, higher ratio of opportunity to competition
