# Director of Technical Product Ownership — ACI Worldwide

Drive technical product strategy and execution for ACI's bill payment products and platforms.

## Responsibilities
- Build and lead high-performing Technical Product Owner teams responsible for platform delivery across the bill payment ecosystem
- Define and drive technical product strategy aligned with product vision, platform architecture, and commercial priorities
- Translate strategic initiatives into scalable platform capabilities, technical requirements, and prioritized delivery roadmaps
- Partner with engineering and architecture leadership on solutions designed for scalability, resilience, security, and operational efficiency
- Establish best practices for product ownership, backlog management, and Agile delivery across multiple engineering teams
- Ensure platform capabilities support the full bill payment lifecycle: presentment, payment orchestration, settlement, reconciliation, reporting, and workflows
- Drive delivery discipline and operational rigor to improve predictability, quality, and velocity

## Required
- 10+ years in product ownership, technical product management, or related roles delivering complex technology platforms
- Proven experience leading Technical Product Owner teams
- Strong background in payments, fintech, or financial services platforms (bill payment, processing, settlement, reconciliation)
- Demonstrated experience delivering and scaling cloud-based, API-driven, SaaS platforms in high-volume environments
- Deep understanding of Agile frameworks, backlog strategy, and cross-team execution

## Preferred
- Direct experience with bill payment platforms or large-scale payment ecosystems
- Hands-on experience with real-time payments, ACH, card networks
- Familiarity with event-driven architectures, microservices, API-first designs
- Highly regulated environment experience
