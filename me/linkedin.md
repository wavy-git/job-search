## About

The way most problems stay problems is that people stop questioning them. They become "how things work." That's usually where the real opportunity is.

My approach to leadership is built on that. Whether it's a product, a team, or an entire operation, the work starts with understanding what's actually happening versus what everyone assumes is happening. From there, it's about building clarity, making decisions visible, and creating an environment where people can do their best work without unnecessary friction.

Having built businesses, products, and teams across startups, Fortune 50, and regulated industries in multiple countries, one thing has proven true everywhere: exceptional teams aren't separated from average ones by ambition. They're separated by imagination, the ability to see what doesn't exist yet and believe they can build it.
