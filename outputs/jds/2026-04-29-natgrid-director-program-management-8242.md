# Director of Program Management (8242) — National Grid (NY Gas Business)

## About
NG hiring Director of Program Management for NY Gas Business. Position can be Upstate or Downstate NY.

## Job Purpose
Lead a team of project managers and a program of initiatives supporting continuous improvement, change management, and capability development within NY Gas Business. Strong leadership, proven portfolio/program management track record, coaching project managers, managing key stakeholders. Reports status to Senior Leaders including forecasted and actual benefits of the portfolio. Identifies new initiatives based on performance.

## Key Accountabilities (verbatim)

1. Influence and shape the development and delivery of a Project/Program Management Office with governance to drive consistency across all projects and programs in NY Gas.
2. Manage a program of initiatives to support continuous improvement, aligning with business objectives, targets and identifying root cause for performance failures. Ensuring effective corrective actions are in place with controls to ensure sustainability of change.
3. Develop and analyze a comprehensive set of leading and lagging cross-functional key performance indicators in support of strategic and operational cross-functional process performance.
4. Provide coaching and mentorship to project managers, fostering a culture of collaboration, innovation, and continuous learning.
5. Provide regular updates to Senior Leaders on program performance, metrics, benefits and new initiatives leveraging data-driven insights and stakeholder feedback ensuring transparency and accountability.
6. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure successful execution of program initiatives, driving operational excellence and business value.
7. Develop and maintain relationships with external partners, vendors, and industry associations to stay informed about emerging trends and best practices.

## Required Qualifications
- Bachelors degree + 10 years proven leadership experience building/managing teams (5+ team members), preferably energy/utilities/related industries
- Excellent communication, ability to influence/inspire cross-functional teams + senior stakeholders
- Translate strategic goals into actionable plans; long-term vision + short-term priorities
- Project management + process improvement methodologies (Lean Six Sigma) **required** — minimum 10 projects, large portfolios of efficiency initiatives **$10M to $250M in savings**
- Valid driver's license + ability to travel to SteerCo and team meetings

## Preferred
- PgMP, PMP, Lean Six Sigma, Prosci or other change management certifications
