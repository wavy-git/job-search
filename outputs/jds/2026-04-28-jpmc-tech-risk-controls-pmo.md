## Description

As a Tech Risk & Controls Lead(PMO) within the Cybersecurity Technology and Controls, Governance Risk and Controls division, the role involves driving successful delivery of complex technology projects and programs while identifying and mitigating compliance and operational risks.

## Responsibilities
- Develop and execute comprehensive project plans, incorporating technical requirements, resource allocation, and timelines to ensure on-time delivery
- Identify and mitigate risks, proactively addressing potential roadblocks
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including engineering, product, and business stakeholders, to define program scope
- Utilize advanced analytical reasoning to assess program performance
- Champion the adoption of agile methodologies and technical solutions, fostering a culture of continuous learning

## Required Qualifications
- 5+ years applied experience in technical program management
- Advanced expertise in stakeholder management
- Advanced experience in utilizing technical fluency, including knowledge of vendor products
- Experience managing resources, budgets, and high-performing teams
- Risk management, technology controls, or cybersecurity environment experience
