# Sr. Product Manager, Tech — Devices and Services FinTech — Amazon

## Description
Amazon's Devices and Services Finance organization seeks a senior PM with expertise in financial planning systems. Role focuses on gathering requirements for financial reporting systems and ensuring accurate systems supporting financial analysis across the global finance organization.

- Develop product vision and strategy
- Manage Fintech products throughout lifecycle
- Identify business and technical requirements
- Prioritize software development requests
- Provide analytical support, prototypes, POCs

## Basic Qualifications
- 5+ years in product/program management, marketing, BD, or technology
- Bachelor's degree
- Roadmap strategy, feature delivery, end-to-end product delivery

## Preferred
- SQL/Excel statistical analysis
- Financial modeling, P&L management, or analysis background
- Financial software development experience
