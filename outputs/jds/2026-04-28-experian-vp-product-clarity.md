# VP, Product — Clarity Services — Experian

Leads product strategy/execution for Experian's Clarity Services (alternative financial services data). Reports to Chief Product & Analytics Officer.

## Responsibilities
- Develop/execute product strategy for Clarity portfolio
- Translate market trends and regulatory changes into vision/roadmap
- Lead full product lifecycle
- Engage directly with lenders, fintechs, alternative financial providers
- Partner with sales, marketing, analytics on adoption/revenue growth
- Build and develop high-performing PM teams

## Required
- 10+ years PM with direct ownership of strategy/delivery in financial services, data, or SaaS
- Building/scaling/commercializing data-driven or analytics products
- Credit risk, alternative data, fraud, financial inclusion preferred
- Regulated, data-intensive environments

## Salary
$225,000 - $325,000
