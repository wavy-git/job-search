# Group Product Manager, Platform — Chime

Lead Platform Product including ChimeCore — "a mission-critical, highly technical system that underpins Chime's core financial products" — enabling speed, reliability, and regulatory compliance at scale.

## Responsibilities
- Own product vision/strategy/roadmap for ChimeCore
- Lead and mentor IC PMs
- Partner with Engineering, Infrastructure, Risk, Compliance, Finance, Operations
- Drive clarity on APIs, internal tooling, data flows, system boundaries
- Balance short-term delivery with long-term architectural investments
- Establish success metrics for platform health, reliability, adoption

## Required
- 8–12+ years PM with significant ownership of internal platforms, infrastructure, or highly technical systems
- Prior experience leading PMs (people leadership or strong team-lead)
- Strong technical fluency
- Proven track record in regulated or high-availability environments (fintech, payments, banking, healthcare, large-scale infrastructure)
